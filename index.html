Physical
Superintelligence
(PSI) Lab

The Physical Superintelligence (PSI) Lab at USC develops next-generation robotic systems grounded in large-scale data, foundation models, and real-world deployment.

We build multimodal data engines, generative training pipelines, and advanced visuomotor policies for humanoids and manipulators. Our work spans 3D scene understanding, whole-body humanoid control, and dexterous manipulation, tightly linking ML algorithms with real hardware.

Our goal is to create physical AI agents that can operate safely and autonomously in everyday human environments.
